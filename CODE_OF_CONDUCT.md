# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

This project follows the Contributor Covenant version 2.0. By participating, you agree to abide by its terms.

Summary

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Be mindful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Harassment and abusive behavior are not tolerated.

Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the maintainers at maintainers@example.org. All reports will be handled confidentially.

For the full text, see: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/0/code_of_conduct/
